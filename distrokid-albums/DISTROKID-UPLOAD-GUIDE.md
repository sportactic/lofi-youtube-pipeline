## DistroKid Steps

For each album:
1. Go to https://distrokid.com/signup (or login)
2. Click "Add new release"
3. Choose "Single" or "Album" (use Album for all)
4. Upload the cover.jpg (3000x3000 required)
5. Upload tracks in order (filename indicates track number)
6. Fill metadata:
   - Release title: Cozy Hours / Matsuri Dreams / Late Hours
   - Artist: TheLoFi / LofiNippon / FlyChill
   - Genre: Lo-Fi Hip Hop / Japanese Lo-Fi
   - Release date: 2026-06-29
   - Copyright: © 2026 [Artist Name]
7. Distribute to: Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon, etc.

## Important Notes

⚠️ Each track is ~135-185 seconds long
⚠️ Tracks 1+3+5+7+... + 2+4+6+8+... are PART I and PART II variations
   - You can either keep both (representing the 2 loops in videos)
   - Or use only Part I (cleaner album for streaming)
⚠️ Album artwork is high quality 3000x3000 (DistroKid spec)
