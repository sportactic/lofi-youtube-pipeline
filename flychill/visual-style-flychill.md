## Composition Rules
- 16:9 aspect ratio, 1920x1080 minimum
- Title-safe area: text in upper-center, leave 200px from top/bottom for YouTube title overlays
- Always include: soft depth-of-field, bokeh lights, atmospheric particles (rain, dust, snow, steam)
- Art style: high-quality digital illustration / 90s anime ambient / Studio Ghibli backgrounds (NOT characters)

## Negative Prompt (do NOT include)
- People/faces/humans (no mascot)
- Bright/saturated colors
- Sharp edges, harsh lighting
- Text, logos, watermarks
- Suno logo, YouTube logo

## Generation Prompt Template
```
{detailed scene description}, soft ambient lighting, cool color palette with deep blue and lavender,
golden accent lights, bokeh city lights, atmospheric particles, peaceful mood,
90s anime background art style, Studio Ghibli ambient, ultra detailed,
16:9 composition, dreamy lo-fi aesthetic --no people faces text
```

## Thumbnail Text Style
- Title overlays use cream/off-white bold serif (Cormorant Garamond or similar)
- Drop shadow for readability
- Optional: "Lofi" or "Chillhop" badge in corner
