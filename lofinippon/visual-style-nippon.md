# LoFi Nippon Visual Style Guide

## Channel: @lofinippon
**Channel ID:** `UCEXLQ3tlpMu4TWnHojfEP7g`

## Primary Character
**Anime girl in kimono** — long straight black hair, kanzashi hairpins, sitting in traditional Japanese settings.

## Secondary Character (mascot)
**Tanuki** (Japanese raccoon dog) — round, friendly, classic lo-fi Nippon mascot. Tanuki motifs are beloved in Japanese culture.

Alternative: **Kitsune** (white fox) for shrine settings.

## Visual Templates

### 1. Sakura Night Walk
- Anime girl in pink/mauve kimono
- Walking under cherry blossom trees at night
- Soft pink petals falling
- Tanuki walking beside her
- Stone lantern glowing warmly
- Moon visible through branches
- **Palette:** Soft pink #FFB7C5, deep indigo #1a1a3a, gold leaf #d4af37, cream paper lantern #f4e8d0

### 2. Tokyo Train Window
- Anime girl with short hair and modern coat
- Inside Yamanote Line train at night
- Rain on window, neon Tokyo lights blurring past
- Tanuki sleeping on the seat beside her
- Cityscape of Shinjuku/Shibuya silhouettes
- **Palette:** Warm amber interior + cool neon teal/electric pink + rain blue

### 3. Zen Garden
- Anime girl in white kimono
- Sitting on stone bench beside raked sand garden
- Tsurukubi (hanging lantern) in background
- Tanuki resting on moss beside her
- Bamboo grove to one side
- **Palette:** Pale jade bamboo + warm cream sand + soft stone grey + cream lantern glow

### 4. Bamboo Forest
- Anime girl with hair flowing
- Standing in mystical bamboo grove
- Soft light filtering through green bamboo
- Tanuki clinging to bamboo stalk nearby
- Distant mountain silhouette
- **Palette:** Deep emerald + pale jade + soft cream highlights

### 5. Tea Ceremony
- Anime girl kneeling in tatami room
- Pouring matcha tea from chawan
- Soft shoji screen light
- Tanuki sitting on zabuton cushion nearby
- Bamboo ikebana arrangement
- **Palette:** Warm tatami cream + soft jade green + tea-foam green

### 6. Torii Gate Path
- Anime girl walking up stone steps
- Vermillion torii gates receding into misty distance
- Tanuki following close behind
- Forest on either side
- Stone lanterns lining the path
- **Palette:** Vermillion red-orange + deep forest green + misty teal + cream stone

### 7. Old Kyoto Alley
- Anime girl in traditional yukata
- Walking through narrow stone-paved alley
- Wooden machiya houses with paper lanterns
- Tanuki poking out from doorway
- Rain-damp cobblestones reflecting lantern glow
- **Palette:** Warm amber lantern + cool teal rain + wood brown + cream paper

### 8. Tsukimi Moon
- Anime girl on engawa (veranda)
- Looking up at full harvest moon
- Susuki grass (pampas) swaying in foreground
- Tanuki sitting beside her, also gazing at moon
- Autumn leaves scattered on ground
- **Palette:** Cream moonlight + deep indigo sky + warm autumn gold + soft cream

### 9. Sake Bar
- Anime girl behind izakaya counter
- Pouring sake from tokkuri into ochoko cups
- Warm paper lantern overhead with kanji
- Tanuki curled up sleeping in the corner
- Wood grain textures everywhere
- **Palette:** Warm amber lantern + dark wood brown + cream sake foam + soft red

### 10. Mount Fuji Dawn
- Anime girl at mountain viewpoint
- Soft pink/red sunrise over snow-capped Fuji
- Tanuki sitting beside her, watching
- Tea steaming in her hands
- **Palette:** Sakura pink dawn + snow white Fuji + soft cream + distant indigo
